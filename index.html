Background

About me

I'm an AI & Deep Learning Scientist at Cynapto Technologies (Mumbai). I am the technical owner of two flagship verticals — agentic AI (enterprise copilots, multi-agent platforms) and visual dubbing (diffusion lip-sync pipeline + editing studio) — taking each from architecture and roadmap planning through design, development, testing, and production rollout.

My work sits at the intersection of cutting-edge research and real deployment. I've trained diffusion-based lip-sync models on 500 hours of visual-speaking data across 4×A100 and 4×H100 clusters, built multi-tenant agentic RAG platforms with LangChain, FAISS, and Pinecone for enterprises, and designed drag-and-drop multi-agent orchestration systems powered by LangGraph and Langflow that run 24×7 with event-driven triggers and human-in-the-loop checkpoints.

I hold an M.Tech in Artificial Intelligence and Robotics from Gautam Buddha University (8.0 GPA) and have published research on Generative Adversarial Networks. I also mentor aspiring AI/ML practitioners and have completed 15+ certifications from Google, IBM, and DeepLearning.ai.

When I'm not building agents, I'm exploring new model architectures, contributing to open-source research, or shipping freelance AI projects internationally.
